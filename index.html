Introduction

Chip is a community-driven meme token built on the Vector Smart Chain (VSC). It empowers users to strategically navigate the volatile crypto market and capitalize on price dips.

While embracing the fun nature of meme tokens, Chip provides real value through innovative tokenomics, a robust roadmap, and an NFT reward system.

Supply Details

Total Supply: 10,000,000 CHIP

4% Transaction Tax

The 4% transaction tax mirrors VSG’s $4 mechanism and is distributed as follows:

  • 1% Burn: Permanently removes tokens from circulation, increasing scarcity.
  • 1% Stakeholders: Distributed proportionally among stakers based on their holdings.
  • 1% Liquidity Pool (LP): Reinjected into the liquidity pool to ensure stability and growth.
  • 1% Development: Supports ongoing development, maintenance, and necessary care.

NFT Rewards

The updated NFT tier system provides unique benefits for community engagement and contribution.

Base Tier

Quantity: 550 NFTs

Benefits:

  • Access to basic community channels.
  • Early notifications about platform updates.
  • Standard staking rewards for in-platform purchases or exclusive content access.

Mid Tier

Quantity: 450 NFTs

Benefits:

  • All benefits from the Base Tier.
  • Eligibility for standard airdrops.
  • Voting rights in minor community polls.
  • Increased staking rewards.

High Tier

Quantity: 350 NFTs

Benefits:

  • Includes all benefits of the lower tiers.
  • Access to exclusive digital events and previews.
  • Greater voting power in community governance.
  • Top-tier staking rewards.

Top Tier

Quantity: 250 NFTs

Benefits:

  • All benefits of previous tiers.
  • One raffle ticket per NFT for bi-yearly airdrop drawings.
  • Exclusive voting rights on critical project decisions.
  • Maximum staking rewards.

Founders Tier

Quantity: 50 NFTs

Benefits:

  • All benefits of previous tiers.
  • Two raffle tickets per NFT for bi-yearly airdrop drawings.
  • Invitations to roundtable discussions with founders.
  • Recognition as a Founding Member.
  • A physical print of their NFT.
  • Significant voice in foundational decisions.
  • Top-level staking rewards including profit-sharing.

Bi-Yearly Airdrop Mechanics:

  • Frequency: Every six months.
  • Eligibility: Exclusive to Top Tier and Founders Tier members.
  • Raffle Tickets: Top Tier holders receive 1 ticket per NFT, Founders Tier holders receive 2 tickets per NFT.
  • Reward Amount: Varies based on $CHIP's current market value at the time of the airdrop.
  • Market Adjustment: Number of $CHIP tokens distributed dynamically reflects the token's market price.
  • Raid Requirement: To qualify for normal airdrops, users must actively participate in community raids, contributing to the growth and engagement of the ecosystem.

Buyback & Burn Mechanism

Chip implements a buyback and burn mechanism linked to VSG’s burn rate. This reduces the circulating supply, ensuring scarcity and value retention.

How It Works:

  • Monitors VSG burn data weekly.
  • Calculates an equivalent amount of CHIP tokens to be burned.
  • Executes buyback and burn operations to maintain value.
