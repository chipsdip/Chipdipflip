CHIP Tokenomics

Total Supply and Distribution

CHIP’s total supply is 500 million tokens, strategically allocated to build a robust ecosystem:

  • 60% Liquidity Pool (Locked): Ensures deep liquidity and market stability.
  • 20% Treasury (AI-Managed & Locked): Funds strategic initiatives, including our AI-driven systems.
  • 9% Development Fund: Supports infrastructure, security audits, and continuous upgrades.
  • 4% Community Outreach: Drives growth through rewards, airdrops, and engagement activities.
  • 2% Creator’s Wallet (Locked for 3 Months): Aligns the project’s long-term vision with creator commitment.

Transaction Tax Mechanics & Deflationary Measures

A 6% transaction tax is applied to every trade, reinforcing a sustainable economic model:

  • 1% Burn: Permanently removes tokens from circulation to enhance scarcity.
  • 1% Redistribution: Rewards current holders and promotes loyalty.
  • 1% Liquidity Contribution: Fortifies market depth and trading conditions.
  • 1% Development Fund: Invests in ongoing innovation and ecosystem growth.
  • 1% Treasury Fund: Supports community-governed projects and strategic initiatives.
  • 1% Creator’s Wallet: Sustains the long-term vision and future development.

In addition, our deflationary model includes weekly burn synchronization tied to network activity. The proprietary Snack Attack AI and Chip Stacker systems automatically monitor and manage treasury operations, ensuring that buybacks and liquidity injections occur in a secure, community-governed manner.

Buyback & Burn Mechanism

CHIP’s deflationary model is enforced by an automated buyback and burn strategy. For every 1,000 VSG burned in the ecosystem, 1 CHIP is also burned—coupling our token’s deflationary mechanics with network security.

Community-led votes determine treasury use, and our AI-driven systems (Snack Attack AI and Chip Stacker) ensure that funds are utilized dynamically—either to reduce supply directly or to bolster liquidity in times of volatility.
