Buyback & Burn Mechanism

Chip implements a buyback and burn mechanism linked to VSG\u2019s burn rate. This reduces the circulating supply, ensuring scarcity and value retention.

How It Works:

  • Monitors VSG burn data weekly.
  • Calculates an equivalent amount of CHIP tokens to be burned.
  • Executes buyback and burn operations to maintain value.
