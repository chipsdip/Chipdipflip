Introduction

Chip is a community-driven meme token built on the Vector Smart Chain (VSC). It empowers users to strategically navigate the volatile crypto market and capitalize on price dips.

While embracing the fun nature of meme tokens, Chip provides real value through innovative tokenomics, a robust roadmap, and an NFT reward system.

Tokenomics

Token Distribution

  • 60% Liquidity Pool
  • 15% CEX Listings
  • 10% Buyback & Burn
  • 10% Developers
  • 5% Community Building

Supply Details

Total Supply: 10,000,000 CHIP

4% Transaction Tax

The 4% transaction tax mirrors VSG’s $4 mechanism and is distributed as follows:

  • 1% Burn: Permanently removes tokens from circulation, increasing scarcity.
  • 1% Stakeholders: Distributed proportionally among stakers based on their holdings.
  • 1% Liquidity Pool (LP): Reinjected into the liquidity pool to ensure stability and growth.
  • 1% Development: Supports ongoing development, maintenance, and necessary care.
