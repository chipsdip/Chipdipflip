CHIP Tokenomics

  • 60% Liquidity Pool (Locked): Provides deep liquidity and maintains market stability.
  • 20% Treasury (AI-Managed & Locked): Funds strategic initiatives, including our advanced AI governance systems.
  • 9% Development Fund: Supports infrastructure enhancements, security audits, and ongoing upgrades.
  • 4% Community Outreach: Drives growth through rewards, airdrops, and engagement programs.
  • 2% Creator’s Wallet (Locked for 3 Months): Ensures alignment between the project’s long-term vision and creator commitment.

Transaction Tax Mechanics & Deflationary Measures

A 6% transaction tax is applied to every trade, supporting sustainable growth and value retention:

  • 1% Burn: Permanently removes tokens to enhance scarcity.
  • 1% Redistribution: Rewards existing holders and encourages long-term commitment.
  • 1% Liquidity Contribution: Strengthens market depth and stabilizes trading conditions.
  • 1% Development Fund: Reinvests in platform innovation and ecosystem expansion.
  • 1% Treasury Fund: Supports community-governed projects and strategic initiatives.
  • 1% Creator’s Wallet: Sustains the long-term vision and future development.

Our deflationary strategy includes weekly burn synchronization linked to network activity. The proprietary Snack Attack AI and Chip Stacker systems automatically oversee treasury operations and manage liquidity scaling, ensuring all actions align with community governance.

AI Governance Systems

At the heart of CHIP’s innovation are two advanced AI systems that drive secure treasury management and dynamic liquidity scaling.

Snack Attack AI: This system continuously monitors treasury transactions, blocking unauthorized transfers and enforcing strict governance compliance. It safeguards community-approved funds, ensuring they are used solely for their intended purposes.

Chip Stacker: Serving as our automated liquidity management engine, Chip Stacker dynamically adjusts buyback cycles and maintains consistently funded liquidity pools—even amid market volatility.

Voting Structure

CHIP’s governance is powered by a robust voting structure that empowers every token holder. Each CHIP token grants one vote, ensuring a decentralized decision-making process.

To propose a new governance initiative, a member must meet the following requirements:

  • Hold at least 2.5 million CHIP
  • Have a minimum of 1.5 million CHIP staked
  • Own at least one NFT from any tier

Additionally, bonus voting power is awarded to staked tokens and NFT tier holders—ranging from a 10% bonus for Base Tier NFTs up to a 100% bonus for Founders Tier NFTs—ensuring balanced and meaningful community participation.

Buyback & Burn Mechanism

CHIP’s deflationary model is enforced by an automated buyback and burn strategy. For every 1,000 VSG burned in the ecosystem, 1 CHIP is also burned—linking token deflation directly to network activity.

Community-led governance determines the allocation of treasury funds. Our AI-driven systems—Snack Attack AI and Chip Stacker—dynamically manage liquidity injections and execute buyback operations in response to market conditions.
