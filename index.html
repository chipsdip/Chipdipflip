NFT Rewards

The updated NFT tier system provides unique benefits for community engagement and contribution.

Base Tier

Quantity: 550 NFTs

Benefits:

  • Access to basic community channels.
  • Early notifications about platform updates.
  • Standard staking rewards for in-platform purchases or exclusive content access.

Mid Tier

Quantity: 450 NFTs

Benefits:

  • All benefits from the Base Tier.
  • Eligibility for standard airdrops.
  • Voting rights in minor community polls.
  • Increased staking rewards.

High Tier

Quantity: 350 NFTs

Benefits:

  • Includes all benefits of the lower tiers.
  • Access to exclusive digital events and previews.
  • Greater voting power in community governance.
  • Top-tier staking rewards.

Top Tier

Quantity: 250 NFTs

Benefits:

  • All benefits of previous tiers.
  • Highest frequency and value of airdrops.
  • Exclusive voting rights on critical project decisions.
  • Maximum staking rewards.

Founders Tier

Quantity: 50 NFTs

Benefits:

  • All benefits of previous tiers.
  • Invitations to roundtable discussions with founders.
  • Recognition as a Founding Member.
  • A physical print of their NFT.
  • Significant voice in foundational decisions.
  • Top-level staking rewards including profit-sharing.

Bi-Yearly Airdrop Mechanics: Exclusive to Top Tier and Founders Tier members, each airdrop will not exceed $150 in value per recipient, adjusted for market price.
