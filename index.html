Buyback & Burn Mechanism

CHIP incorporates a buyback and burn mechanism tightly synchronized with the Vector Smart Chain (VSC). For every 1,000 VSG burned, 1 CHIP is burned, reducing supply and enhancing value. The mechanism is supported by a community-governed wallet funded with 10% of the initial supply, ensuring adaptive market dynamics.

  • Weekly synchronization with VSG burn data.
  • Community votes to allocate buyback funds for burns or liquidity enhancements.
  • Long-term deflationary strategy to maintain token scarcity.
